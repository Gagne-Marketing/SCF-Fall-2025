My Projects

Tech Assist For Seniors

Tech Assist For Seniors is an in-home technology support service designed for older adults in Lakewood Ranch, FL. The goal of this project was to create a site that is simple, easy to navigate, and welcoming for seniors and caregivers. Built on WordPress, the website highlights the many services offered, including computer help, smartphone setup, and streaming guidance. Alongside the main pages, local SEO strategies were applied to ensure visibility in Manatee and Sarasota counties. Blog posts were added to answer common tech questions, making the site both a resource hub and a marketing tool.

Gagne Marketing

Gagne Marketing showcases local SEO and digital marketing expertise. The site was developed in WordPress to highlight services, client results, and a clear call-to-action for business owners seeking growth. A clean design was paired with keyword-focused service pages that help attract clients across Lakewood Ranch and surrounding areas. In addition, the site incorporates a blog strategy, publishing posts about keyword research, Google Business Profile tips, and competitor analysis. These supporting articles strengthen SEO and position Gagne Marketing as a trusted resource for small businesses.

Mobile Massage and Healing Services

This wellness site was created for a licensed massage therapist serving clients across Lakewood Ranch, FL. Built on WordPress, the design reflects a calming, professional aesthetic with service packages, detailed descriptions, and a strong focus on booking options. Local SEO optimizations target “in-home massage” and “Lakewood Ranch massage services,” ensuring the site reaches the right audience. Supporting blog posts on topics like stress relief, active stretching, and self-care routines were added to engage readers and improve search rankings. The combination of thoughtful design and SEO helped the business establish credibility and attract new clients.
