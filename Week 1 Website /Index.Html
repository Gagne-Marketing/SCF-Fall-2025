About Me

My name is Sean Gagne, and I’m a WordPress and SEO specialist from Manatee County, Florida. I build websites that are simple, effective, and designed to help local businesses grow online. What began as a hobby became a passion after I created my first business site and saw one of my pages reach #1 in Manatee County.

Today, I keep learning through projects and courses at State College of Florida. My focus is on web design, local SEO, and tools that make websites easier to use. My goal is to create clear, optimized sites that connect businesses with more customers.
